her majesty

Akshay Kanthi
back in your arms
mother
my heart skips
a beat as if
to take a pause
and inhale you fully
once and for all
as mind races
to admire you
with its limited
vocabulary
sssshh... ssshhh
i'm silenced
by your majesty
leaving nothing
but an endless
dumbstruck gaze
